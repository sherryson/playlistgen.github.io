CS3200 Project

CS3200 Project; Web page that auto-generates playlists containing music information, where user's can edit the given playlist.

Welcome to Alex and Sherry's auto playlist generator

Our automatic playlist generator allows you to discover new music by randomly creating playlists for you from a public database. Anyone can upload song information to the database, and you can personalize your playlist by manually re-ordering, adding, and removing songs.

Authors and Contributors
